Welcome to pygdv

Presentation

This is a template to develop a Turbogears application, with Tequila already enabled.

  1. Create more pages

    Add more pages in pygdv.templates package.

  2. Use already made templates.

    Some template are presents in pygdv.template.definitions (menu bar, simple form, item grid,...). Feel free to use them and add more.

  3. Get help

    Go and check Turbogears official documentation, official IRC. Take a look at Users, Groups and Permissions it will help you to understand the basics .
